Analytics & Insights

Insights combine real behavioral data with simulated outcomes to provide clear performance signals, early warning alerts, and guidance for operational decisions.

Overview

A unified reporting layer aligns acquisition, activation, retention, and conversion metrics across channels and product journeys.

Funnel analysis

Stage-by-stage drop-off tracking identifies friction points and quantifies expected gain from targeted optimization initiatives.

Cohort tracking

User cohorts grouped by channel, signup period, and intent profile reveal retention quality and long-term value patterns.

Anomaly detection

Rule-based and trend-threshold checks surface unusual KPI shifts early, reducing delayed reaction risk during campaign cycles.

Decision-making impact

  • Prioritizes high-impact experiments with confidence intervals informed by both observed and simulated behavior.
  • Improves communication between growth, product, and leadership teams through shared metric definitions.
  • Accelerates response time when funnel degradation or acquisition quality shifts are detected.
